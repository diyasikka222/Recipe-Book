RAJMA MASALA

Recipe Type : Veg

Ingredients Required :

  • 1 cup red kidney beans (rajma)
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped (or 1 cup tomato purée)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cream (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves, finely chopped

Preparation Steps :

  1. Rinse the red kidney beans under running water. Soak them in enough water for at least 8 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the soaked rajma. In a pressure cooker, add the soaked rajma, 4 cups of water, salt, and turmeric powder. Cook on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes (or until they are soft and cooked through). Once cooked, set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat the oil or ghee. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies to the pan and sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes (or tomato purée) to the pan. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  6. Add red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook the masala for another 5-7 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked rajma along with its cooking water to the pan with the masala. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  8. Simmer the rajma on low heat for at least 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally. You can adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed. The longer you simmer, the better the flavors develop.
  9. If using, add the fresh cream to the rajma and mix well.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with steamed rice, jeera rice, or roti.
Enjoy your homemade Rajma Masala!
